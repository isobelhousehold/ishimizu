ishimizu — 石水

Borosilicate glass bottles paired with gemstones in dedicated chambers. Inspired by Japan’s nature and the idea of ishi-mizu — “stone water”.

Some people believe gemstones carry qualities that can influence their surroundings. In Ishimizu bottles, stones are not in direct contact with the beverage and can be changed easily.

No medical or health claims are made. Enjoy the ritual and design that resonates with you.
