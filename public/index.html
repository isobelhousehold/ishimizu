石水 — ishi‑mizu: “stone water”

Borosilicate bottles, gemstones & mindful hydration

Inspired by Japan’s nature and craft, Ishimizu bottles pair high‑quality borosilicate glass with gemstones held in a separate chamber—never in contact with your drink.

Three models, three intentions

The gemstones are enclosed separately from the beverage.

Heiwa — 平和

Heiwa (peace)

  • Single‑wall borosilicate glass
  • Bell‑shaped chamber with gemstone gravels
  • Lightweight & minimalist

Gemstones: amethyst, rose quartz, rock crystal, etc.

Kanjo — 感情

Kanjo (emotion)

  • Single‑wall borosilicate
  • Gemstone pebbles in the base
  • Balanced feel & everyday use

Gemstones: rose quartz, aventurine, citrine, amethyst, etc.

Hogo — 保護

Hogo (protection)

  • Double‑wall glass for insulation
  • Gemstone pebbles in the base
  • Comfortable grip, reduced condensation
  • Tea filter included

Gemstones: black tourmaline, rock crystal, amethyst, etc.
